Sensors Guide

Sensors Needed

PH Sensor: Measures the acidity or alkalinity of water

TDS Sensor: Measures the total dissolved solids in water

Turbidity Sensor: Measures the clarity of water


Usage Details

To use these sensors, place each sensor in 250 ml of water and read the readings displayed on the meter
Note these readings and use them as input for the classification

Make sure the sensors are properly calibrated before use to ensure accurate readings
Submerge each sensor in the water sample without touching the sides of the container to avoid any interference.
Wait for the readings to stabilize before recording the values. It is recommended to take multiple readings to ensure consistency and accuracy
